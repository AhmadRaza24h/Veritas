About Veritas

Veritas is a data-driven local news analytics platform designed to bring clarity, context, and structure to incident-based news reporting.

Our Mission

Local news often reaches readers through a single source or without analytical context, making it difficult to assess credibility or understand the broader picture. Veritas exists to solve this problem.

Our mission is to help readers see the full picture by aggregating multiple reports of the same incident and presenting structured insights instead of opinions.

What Veritas Does

  • Aggregates local news articles from multiple independent sources
  • Groups related articles into a single tracked incident
  • Analyzes incidents using transparent, rule-based logic
  • Visualizes credibility, perspective distribution, and patterns

Veritas does not generate opinions, headlines, or political commentary. It focuses entirely on structured analysis.

Analysis Philosophy

Veritas is built on the principle that analysis should be explainable. Every score and visualization is derived from clearly defined rules.

  • No sentiment analysis or opinion mining
  • No machine learning black boxes
  • No editorial bias or subjective judgments

Reporting perspectives are classified using predefined source metadata: Public, Neutral, and Political.

Credibility & Perspective

Each incident analyzed by Veritas receives a credibility score based on:

  • Source diversity (40%) – independent reporting across outlets
  • Location clarity (30%) – clear and consistent geographic information
  • Completeness (30%) – presence of key factual details

Perspective distribution highlights how coverage is divided among public-focused, neutral, and political reporting styles.

Technology & Architecture

Veritas is built as a production-ready web application using modern, maintainable architecture principles.

  • Flask with Blueprint and Factory patterns
  • PostgreSQL with SQLAlchemy ORM
  • Service-layer–driven backend logic
  • Bootstrap 5 and Chart.js for visualization

All analysis logic lives on the backend. The frontend is responsible only for presentation.

Academic & Design Context

Veritas is designed as an analysis-focused system rather than a content platform. It emphasizes data structures, relational modeling, and rule-based evaluation.

The project avoids subjective interpretation and is suitable for academic, technical, and demonstrative use cases.

Contributing & Feedback

Veritas is under active development and welcomes structured contributions from team members.

  • News data collection and source expansion
  • Analysis logic improvements
  • Frontend UI refinement
  • Database and performance optimization
