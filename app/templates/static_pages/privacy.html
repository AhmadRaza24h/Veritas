Privacy Policy

This page explains how Veritas collects, uses, and protects user information.

Veritas is designed with privacy as a core principle. The platform collects only the minimum data required to deliver analytical features.

We do not sell personal data, track users for advertising, or perform behavioral profiling.

  • Account information: Username or email (if account creation is enabled)
  • Usage data: News articles viewed, incident categories accessed
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and device metadata (non-identifying)

  • Generate personalized news recommendations
  • Improve platform functionality and analysis quality
  • Maintain system security and prevent misuse
  • Debug errors and monitor performance

Veritas does not use collected data for advertising or marketing.

Veritas does not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

Data may be processed by trusted infrastructure providers strictly for hosting, storage, and system operation.

  • User data is retained only as long as necessary
  • Viewing history may be stored to improve recommendations
  • Inactive user data may be periodically cleaned

Veritas uses reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect user data.

While no system can guarantee absolute security, best practices are followed to minimize risk.

  • Request access to stored personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of account and associated data

Veritas uses cookies or local storage strictly for authentication and session management.

Cookies are not used for tracking or advertising.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the platform evolves or legal requirements change.

Significant updates will be communicated through the platform.
